How to cook Shakshoka

Presenting your Shakshoka pan after a not so hard process of what you might call cooking, since making this delecious dish is as easy as just throwing the ingredients in. Through all the mediterranean cultures shakshoka was always present as an easy to make dish with ingredients always present within the regions' homes

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Eggs
  • Parsley
  • Salt and black pepper
  • gound beef (optional)
