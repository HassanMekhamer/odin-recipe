Bakes Potatoes with Hummus

This dish is here only to satisfy most of your needs when hunger calls and you have less ingredients -or maybe less will power- than usual. Baking your potatoes and crushing your hummus is the most work you will be doing when preparing this dish

Ingredients

  • Several big potatoes
  • Half a Kilo of hummus
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper
  • paprika
  • shatta
